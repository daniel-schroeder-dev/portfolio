I’m a full-stack freelance web developer and educator. I discovered my love for programming in my late 20's, and have spent most of my time since then learning how to build things with code. Although I'm mostly self-taught, I have had help from a few college courses, bootcamps, and dozens of tutorials and books. I've worked with JavaScript, Ruby, Java, C, and PHP, in addition to HTML and CSS.

I’m interested in learning more about UI/UX design, and would love a chance to work closely with graphic designers on implementing amazing client experiences. I also really enjoy writing server-side code, and am hoping to pursue some dev-ops projects in the near future.

I'm currently hard at work developing numerous personal projects as well as teaching the next generation of young developers over at CodeWizardsHQ.

Check out my projects or head over to my GitHub if you want to see some examples of my work, and feel free to contact me if you'd like to work together or have an opportunity I may be interested in.

Recent Projects

Here's a few things I've been working on lately. I keep this list curated with my most recent projects, mainly to keep me honest and building new things constantly!

Many are hosted on free Heroku dynos, so please be patient for the first page load as the servers sleep after 30 minutes of inactivity. All of them should be mobile-friendly unless otherwise noted.

todoList

April, 2020

https://daniel-schroeder-dev.github.io/todo-list-react/

I've probably built 100 todo list apps at this point, but it's always a good test of a new framework or concept to throw one together. I built it without a framework first here.
